Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 1/2 tsp cornstarch
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup butter, melted
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 granulated sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 1 1/4 cups chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F and line baking sheets with parchment paper
  2. In a large bowl, whisk flour, baking soda, cornstarch, and salt together
  3. In a medium bowl, whisk melted butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar together
  4. Whisk in eggs and vanilla
  5. Pour wet mix into dry mix and mix together
  6. Fold in chocolate chips
  7. Spoon cookie dough onto baking sheets
  8. Bake for 12-13 minutes, or until edges are lightly browned
  9. Let cool on baking sheets for 10 minutes, then transfer to wire rack
