Bran Muffins

Description

An easy bran muffin recipe, perfect snacks for kids and adults alike. High in fibre and delicious. Ready in 30 minutes. Yields 12 muffins.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups wheat bran
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 2/3 cup brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla
  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup raisins, optional

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F
  2. In a large bowl, combine bran and buttermilk. Let stand for 5 minutes
  3. Stir in vegetable oil, brown sugar, egg, and vanilla
  4. In a seperate bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt
  5. Add bran mixture and stir until combined
  6. Gently fold in raisins and spoon into lined muffin tin
  7. Bake 15-20 minutes
