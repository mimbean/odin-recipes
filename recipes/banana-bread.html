Banana Bread

Description

Sweet, moist, banana-y! A great loaf to share with your aunts and siblings. Enjoy this loaf in the morning or around the evening fire. Great with butter or jam. Stores well in the fridge. Ready in 1 hour and 15 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 1/3 cups mashed overripe bananas

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F
  2. Lightly grease load pan
  3. Combine flour, baking soda, and salt in a large bowl
  4. In a seperate bowl, beat brown sugar and butter together until smooth
  5. Stir in eggs and mashed bananas until blended
  6. Stir banana mix into flour mix and pour into loaf pan
  7. Bake in oven until an inserted toothpick comes out clean, which is about 60 minutes
  8. Let cool for 10 minutes in pan, and then completely on a wire rack
